Hack The Future 24

Hackathon hosted by Microsoft for Young Adults

On June 22nd, the Bay Area Youth Computer Science Council helped run the Hack The Future 24. Zachary the Technical Director of BAYCSC managed over 200 students. He also helped out by mentoring at HTML and Scratch throughout the Hackathon and helping them find stations that they are passionate about.

Jessica, Supriya, and Zachary then helped out in the Hackathon mentoring in Scratch (block code), specifically tailored to kids from age 6 - 12. The BAYCSC made a lesson plan for the students to follow tailored to whether they were beginner, intermediate, and advanced.
